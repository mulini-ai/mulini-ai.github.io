Mulini

Mulini is an EU-based SME, specializing in research and development on security & privacy of cyber physical systems. Mulini’s solutions are built upon years of extensive research and open source product contributions on security, privacy, and auditing tools for Internet of Things (IoT) consumer, industrial, and medical devices and their applications. Mulini’s range of offerings include consumer and industrial IoT security solutions. Using one of the world's most advanced and widely-recognized IoT testbeds, we develop AI-based threat detection and cybersecurity tools for protecting critical infrastructure against cyber security attacks, and auditing tools for device certification and regulatory compliance.
